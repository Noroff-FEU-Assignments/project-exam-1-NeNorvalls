Hey, I am Ettenytten.

I will tour you in the world of Filipino's. Hope you will enjoy reading my blog!

I am a pure Filipina that is living in Norway. I am currently studying front-end development. I like posting blogs about my daily adventures and experiences. I am still being a Filipina kind both in nature and mindset. I have two young girls that I prefer to raise in a Filipina way. I love my country and am proud to share some of how we are in there or what we are known as. Filipinos are all around the world, and it was nice to be one among them. This blog focuses on viewing how we Filipinos are in our own way. Loads of changes and happenings in our lives curves us into who we are now. Let us bring the old ways and new era together! Life is full of fun and adventure to share with you, so I invited you to know a bit about us! Feel free to contact us or send a comment. Thank you!
